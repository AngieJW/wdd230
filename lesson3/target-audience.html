Target Audience

Cassis Chamber of Commerce is mainl for local business owners and entrepreneurs. Age range from 20 to 65. Cassis town is located on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea in the Provence region of southern France. Therefore the tourism industry is the most developed. The main of the audience will be business owners of hotels, bed&breakfasts, restaurants, souvenirs shops, boat rental shops, as wel as of businesses offering water-based activities or outdoor experiences. The audience is also local small business owners of town shops, like bakery, hair salon, beauty salon, art gallery, and market vendors.

Personas

William and Grace Hart

The Harts are Bed & Breakfast owners in their fifties. The love to get involved in the communit, the want to know about all the events and the are looking to create partnerships with other local business owners.

Noah Lucas

Noah is a 30year-old entrepreneur who fell in love with the town and wants to launch a new venture here. He loves the outdoor and has a diploma in sales and management, he is considering opening either a paddle rental shop or a hiking gear shop. He wants information about the competition, he also wants to network and find resources to start his business.

Scenarios

  • What is the next community event I can attend to network?
  • Are there educational workshops for professional development being held in the town?
  • Is there a small business I can partner with to expand my client database?
  • Can I find a tax law firm around me?
  • Who can help me in my hiring process?
